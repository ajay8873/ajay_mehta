Medical Journey & Training

I am a final-year MBBS student currently undergoing clinical rotations at Sheikh Bhikhari Medical College (formerly known as Hazaribagh Medical College), located in Hazaribag, Jharkhand. My medical education emphasizes hands-on patient care, clinical reasoning, and emergency case management.

In 2022, I cleared the competitive NEET-UG examination on my very first attempt with a score of 613 marks, while simultaneously completing my Class 12th board exams. This transition from foundational school education at *Saraswati Shishu Vidya Mandir* and *Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Palamu* laid the bedrock of discipline and analytical thinking required for medicine.

Clinical Rotations Emergency Care Diagnostics Patient History Medical Education

Tech & Web Projects

Beyond the clinical wards, I am a passionate tech hobbyist and software developer. I build functional web utilities and interactive applications that solve daily problems or support medical students at the point of care.

My primary tech stack includes JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5/CSS3, and Supabase (DB & Auth). I design offline-first applications utilizing browser caching (Service Workers, cookies, and LocalStorage) to keep utilities fully accessible in clinical settings where internet connectivity is often limited.

Vanilla JavaScript HTML5 / CSS3 Supabase DB Service Worker / PWA Front-end Design

Hobbies & Philosophy

To maintain a balanced lifestyle, I dedicate time to creative outlets such as anatomical sketching, illustrating cardiovascular and systemic organ anatomy. I am also an avid reader, having completed classic works by Munshi Premchand (*Godan*, *Nirmala*), Dharamvir Bharati (*Gunaho Ka Devta*), Vinod Kumar Shukla, Franz Kafka (*Metamorphosis*), Fyodor Dostoevsky (*White Nights*), and Kanta Bharati (*Ret Ki Machhali*).

When resting between postings, I enjoy listening to acoustic songs by Arijit Singh, Papon, Jagjit Singh, and Kishore Kumar, and look forward to embarking on budget backpacking journeys post-graduation.
